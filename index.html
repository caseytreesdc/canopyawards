The Canopy Awards

The Canopy Awards is Casey Trees’ flagship event that highlights the accomplishments of those working to boost and protect D.C.’s tree canopy, raises funds and gets you out for a night of fun. In past years, we got campy under the canopy, celebrated our beautiful parks and followed Alice into the canopy.
